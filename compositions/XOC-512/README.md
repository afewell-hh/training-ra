# XOC‑512 — Overview

This tier composes OPG units behind a DS5000 spine to reach 512 xPUs. We provide two bundles:

Bundles
- 1x-OPG-512/ — one OPG‑512 under a spine (demonstrates XOC structure at this size)
- 2x-OPG-128/ — two OPG‑128 units under a spine

Notes
- Variants mirror the underlying OPGs (rail‑optimized and dual‑plane for OPG‑512; rail‑optimized and single‑homed for OPG‑128).
